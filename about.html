Summary About Me

Thabile is an author, public speaker, and mentor. Thabile holds a BA degree as a major in Psychology from University of Johannesburg formerly known as Vista University. Thabile spent her professional career working in the corporate world for a well-known financial advisory & insurance institution. As a survivor of domestic marital GBV, she is passionate about empowering woman and addressing the issues relating to women and children abuse. She is a born-again Christian and child of God, she is also an active member of her local church. She serves under the intercession department as a prayer warrior. Most important of all she is a mother and aims to provide her children with good guidance and support through life.
